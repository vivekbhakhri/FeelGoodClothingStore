About Us

As we have analyzed now the world become digital. So, we decided to make website of clothing store which is known as “FeelGood” where customer can easily buy clothes, bags and shoes on internet instead of using traditional way of purchasing clothing stuff via going to shops. I observed it is more cumbersome method which is used to very time consuming. With the help of “FeelGood” app consumer are able to acquire clothing products at anytime and anywhere. This app provides the facility of clients to register in it and after registration they can login with their account, in addition to it, masses can select the items in add to cart then they can buy through online transaction. Moreover, admin login is available where they are able to add category, add products and sales. Besides this, users can view history regarding orders admin to and admin can confirm the orders.

I hope this project would be overcome the genuine problem of clients in an efficient manner regarding to obtain paint online. This is all about of my project!
